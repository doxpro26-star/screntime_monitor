## 🎯 What's Next: Deploy to Railway (5 minutes)

Now that your code is on GitHub, deploying to Railway is super easy!

### Step 1: Go to Railway
https://railway.app

### Step 2: Sign Up with GitHub
- Click "Sign Up" (top right)
- Select "GitHub" login
- Authorize Railway

### Step 3: Create New Project
- Click "Create New Project"
- Select "Deploy from GitHub repo"

### Step 4: Select Your Repository
- Search for: `screntime_monitor`
- Click on your repo
- Railway auto-detects Python project

### Step 5: Deploy
- Railway reads your `Procfile`
- Railway reads your `requirements.txt`
- Click "Deploy"
- Wait 2-5 minutes

### Step 6: Access Your App
- Railway gives you a URL (like `https://app-xyz.railway.app`)
- Open in browser
- See your dashboard! 🎉
